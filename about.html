About Us

Lipe-Portugal is a premier textile sourcing agency with around 30 years of expertise in the industry. Founded in 1997 and based in Portugal's textile heartland of Barcelos, we specialize in connecting international brands with high-quality Portuguese manufacturers for socks, sportswear, and workwear.

Our deep understanding of textile manufacturing, combined with strong local partnerships, allows us to deliver exceptional products with competitive pricing and reliable lead times. We serve clients across Europe and the Middle East, building long-term relationships based on trust and consistent quality.

What sets us apart is our hands-on approach - we work closely with both clients and manufacturers throughout the entire production process, ensuring every detail meets your specifications. From material selection to final quality control, we manage the entire supply chain so you don't have to.
